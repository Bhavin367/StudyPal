What is StudyPal?

StudyPal combines a simple notepad with a Pomodoro timer to help you study efficiently. You can create notes on different subjects and manage your study sessions with focus and break intervals.

Section Pages

  • Notepad: Write and save notes for the selected subject. Changes auto-save to your browser.
  • Pomodoro Timer: Use the timer to start focused study sessions with default 25 minutes work and 5 minutes break intervals.
  • Modes: Switch between Pomodoro mode and Deep Study mode. Deep Study mode offers longer sessions for more concentration.
  • Session Tracking: Your completed sessions are logged per subject for progress tracking.
